Quirks and Obsessions

You see, we all need a little escape from reality, right? While some folks unwind by counting sheep, I prefer to dive headfirst into the whimsical worlds of art and music, because, well, who needs sleep anyway? As one of my many "About Me" identities will tell you, I'm a bona fide classical music aficionado who tinkled the ivories on the piano in my youth. My most prized non-living possession, apart from my health insurance, is a Kawaii digital piano that I splurged on as a self-congratulatory gift for quitting my last job. It's like my personal confetti cannon of achievement! Now, I may not quite practice 40 hours a day (and if you get that joke, you're my kind of person because we love the same specific Youtube channel), but I've got a knack for keeping busy.
